I aim to capture the essence of a brief, descriptive website that shows not only technical skill but also my personality itself to anyone who may not know me. My first prototype

Home page.

This is my first portfolio page. It was very basic and I had little understanding of essential tools such as flexbox and css grid, however for a first portfolio it was rather exciting to launch and call my own. Where it lacks in functionality, it gave back in character as it was my first design and I particularly enjoyed the overlayed design on my about page where the content would lay over one another like a stack of notes. For my current website I chose to go for a more simple design, however I am still a large fan of how I formatted my old one.
This website also featured a non-functional projects and contact page, with working buttons for my social media accounts to contact me.
In recent times, I have been learning how to better create forms and soon to learn to make a functional email submission form.
